Key highlights:

- Customer retention rate improved to 96.3%
- Launched 3 new AI features driving upsell revenue
- Completed acquisition of DataStream Technologies for $28 million
- Expanded engineering team by 45 new hires

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

New Features Launched in Q4:
1. AI Anomaly Detection Engine (October 2025)
   - Automatically detects statistical anomalies across all data sources
   - Uses unsupervised learning with isolation forests and autoencoders
   - Reduced false positive rate by 67% compared to rule-based alerts
   - Adopted by 340 customers within first 60 days
   - Generated $1.8M in upsell revenue

2. Natural Language Query Interface (November 2025)
   - Allows users to ask questions in plain English
   - Translates to SQL/analytics queries using fine-tuned LLM
   - Supports 12 languages including Mandarin, Spanish, and Arabic
   - 92% query accuracy rate in user testing
   - Average query response time: 1.3 seconds
   - 1,200 active users within first 30 days

3. Predictive Dashboard Builder (December 2025)
   - AI suggests optimal dashboard layouts based on data characteristics
   - Auto-generates forecasts for any time-series metric
   - One-click report generation with executive summaries
   - Integration with Slack, Teams, and email for scheduled reports

RISK FACTORS

1. Competitive Landscape: Increasing competition from Snowflake, Databricks, and Microsoft Fabric. Mitigation: Accelerating AI feature development and deepening vertical specialization.

2. Macroeconomic Uncertainty: Potential enterprise spending slowdown in 2026. Mitigation: Diversifying into mid-market segment and government contracts.

3. Talent Retention: High demand for AI/ML engineers driving up compensation. Mitigation: Enhanced equity refreshers, flexible work policies, and internal training programs.

4. Regulatory Risk: Emerging AI regulations in EU (AI Act) and potential US federal legislation. Mitigation: Proactive compliance team established, participating in industry working groups.

5. Concentration Risk: Top 20 customers represent 18% of revenue. Mitigation: Expanding customer base through mid-market push and geographic diversification.
